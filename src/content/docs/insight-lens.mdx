<section class="app-section">
  <h2>What it does</h2>
  <div class="app-features">
    <div class="app-feature">
      <h3>Import PDF surveys</h3>
      <p>Drop in unit survey PDF reports and extract the data automatically — no manual re-keying or spreadsheet wrangling.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-feature">
      <h3>Rich visualisations</h3>
      <p>Charts for trends over semesters, comparisons across units, and breakdowns that actually make survey data legible.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-feature">
      <h3>Privacy-first</h3>
      <p>Everything stays on your computer. No cloud upload, no account, no telemetry — your survey data never leaves your machine.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-feature">
      <h3>AI assistant</h3>
      <p>Optional AI-powered analysis using your own API key. Ask questions about your data and get summaries in plain English.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-feature">
      <h3>Local SQLite database</h3>
      <p>Your data lives in a SQLite file you control. Inspect it, back it up, or query it directly — no vendor lock-in.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-feature">
      <h3>Cloud-sync ready</h3>
      <p>Point the database at a Dropbox, iCloud, or OneDrive folder and your survey history follows you across machines.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="app-section">
  <h2>How it works</h2>
  <div class="app-steps">
    <div class="app-step">
      <h3>Import your PDFs</h3>
      <p>Drag unit survey reports into Insight Lens and let it extract the numbers, comments, and metadata for you.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-step">
      <h3>Explore the data</h3>
      <p>Browse units, semesters, and question categories with interactive charts and filters.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-step">
      <h3>Ask the AI</h3>
      <p>Bring your own API key and ask questions like "what changed since last semester?" or "summarise the open comments".</p>
    </div>
    <div class="app-step">
      <h3>Track over time</h3>
      <p>Keep a running history in a local database you own — watch trends emerge across years of surveys.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
